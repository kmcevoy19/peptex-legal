Privacy Policy

PepTex — Effective Date: June 24, 2026 This Privacy Policy describes how PepTex ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the PepTex mobile application (the "App"). PepTex is a personal research tracking tool. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below. 1. Information We Collect Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and authentication credentials. We do not store passwords in plain text. User-Generated Content: Information you enter into the App, including: Peptide protocols, dose logs, and tracking entries Daily wellness check-in data (energy, sleep, mood, pain, recovery) Body weight, lab results, and other health metrics you choose to log Progress photos you upload Notes, vendor information, and Certificate of Analysis (COA) data Apple Health Integration (Optional): If you enable HealthKit integration, the App reads weight and sleep data from Apple Health and may write weight data back. This data stays on your device. Usage Data: The App collects anonymous, non-identifying usage analytics (see "Anonymous Usage Analytics" below) and may log technical information (crashes, errors) to improve reliability. 2. How We Use Your Information To provide the App's core functionality (tracking, calculations, reminders) To generate AI-powered research protocol suggestions based on data you submit (processed via secure third-party AI services) To authenticate your account and sync data across devices (when enabled) To send local notifications for dose reminders and check-ins To understand which features are used so we can improve the App (via anonymous analytics) 3. Data Storage Your tracking data is primarily stored locally on your device. If you sign in with an account, data may be backed up to our secure servers (Supabase) for cross-device sync. Apple Health data is never transmitted off your device. 4. Third-Party Services PepTex uses the following third-party services: Supabase for account authentication and optional cloud sync Anthropic Claude API for AI-generated research protocol suggestions (data is sent securely and not used for AI training) Apple HealthKit for optional health data integration (data stays on your device) RevenueCat for subscription management (processes payment information via Apple) TelemetryDeck for anonymous, privacy-preserving usage analytics (no personal or health data) 5. Anonymous Usage Analytics We use TelemetryDeck for privacy-focused, anonymous usage analytics — counts of which features are used so we can improve the App. These signals contain no personally identifying information, account details, or peptide/dose/health data. Data is processed in a privacy-preserving way and is not used to track you across apps or services. You can opt out at any time in Profile → Privacy → "Share Anonymous Usage Data." 6. AI-Generated Content The App uses artificial intelligence to generate research protocol suggestions, peptide analysis, and outcome correlations. Information you submit to AI features (quiz responses, dose logs, check-in data) is processed by Anthropic's Claude API. This data is transmitted securely and is not used to train AI models. 7. Data Sharing We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your tracking data with third parties except as needed to provide the App's services (e.g., AI processing for research suggestions). We may disclose information if required by law. 8. Your Rights You can: Access and edit your data within the App Export your data via the Profile → Export feature (CSV, PDF, JSON formats) Delete your data via Profile → Delete All Data Delete your account by contacting us or using the in-app delete account option Opt out of anonymous usage analytics in Profile → Privacy 9. Children's Privacy PepTex is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we learn we have collected information from a minor, we will delete it. 10. Security We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials secure. 11. Important Health Disclaimer PepTex is a personal research tracking tool. It is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. All information provided in the App is for educational and research reference purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before using any peptide or making health decisions. 12. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through the App or via email. 13.
