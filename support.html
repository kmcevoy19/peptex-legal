Frequently Asked Questions How does the reconstitution calculator work? Enter your vial size (mg of peptide), the amount of bacteriostatic water you're adding, and your target dose. PepTex calculates how many units to draw on an insulin syringe and shows a visual guide. Always verify calculations independently. Where is my data stored? Your tracking data is stored on your device. If you create an account, your data can be securely synced across your devices. Apple Health data never leaves your phone. See our Privacy Policy for full details. How do I manage or cancel my subscription? PepTex Pro subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. To cancel or change your subscription, open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, tap Subscriptions, and select PepTex. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. How do I restore my purchase? Open PepTex, go to Profile → Subscription, and tap "Restore Purchases." This will restore any active subscription associated with your Apple ID. How do I export my data? Go to Profile → Export My Data. You can export your logs and tracking data in CSV, PDF, or JSON format. How do I delete my data or account? Go to Profile → scroll to the bottom. You can delete all your local data or delete your account entirely. For complete server-side account deletion, email us at support@peptex.app and we'll process your request. Is PepTex medical advice? No. PepTex is a personal research tracking and educational tool. It is not a medical device, does not provide medical advice, and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider before using any peptide or making health decisions. How do I turn off analytics? Go to Profile → Privacy and toggle off "Share Anonymous Usage Data." PepTex uses privacy-preserving analytics that never include personal or health data, but you can opt out at any time. Still need help?
